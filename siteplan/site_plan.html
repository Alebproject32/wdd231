Reason

I chose this website project because I am halfway to getting my degree in Telecommunication Engineering and it's a way to get a landscape about my major and one of the future profession.

Site Purpose

The site or page will offer information about the three most significant areas of Telecommunication Engineering that one should know before venturing into this world of applied sciences.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: As a high school student considering a career in engineering, I want to understand what Telecommunication Engineering entails and if it's the right path for me. What are the core subjects I'll study, and what kind of jobs can I expect after graduation?
  • Scenario 2: I'm an aspiring engineer looking for reliable information on the key branches of Telecommunication Engineering. Where can I find concise explanations of areas like wireless communication, optical fiber, and network protocols, and how do they interconnect?

Color Scheme

I would like colors related to telecommunications, one of these colors is dark blue and another lighter blue, and white font color.

  • Dark Blue (#00008B): Used for main headings (h1, h2), potentially as a background for certain sections or footers.
  • Light Blue (#ADD8E6): Used for subheadings (h3), accent elements, or borders.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for paragraph text and list items to ensure readability against dark backgrounds.

Typography

  • Headings (h1, h2, h3): 'Segoe UI', Arial, sans-serif (clean, modern, and readable for titles).
  • Body Text (paragraphs, list items): 'Open Sans', Verdana, sans-serif (highly readable and suitable for longer blocks of text).
