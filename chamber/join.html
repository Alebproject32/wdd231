NP Membership Benefits (Non-Profit)

  • Access to basic networking events.
  • Inclusion in the chamber's non-profit directory listing.
  • Monthly newsletter subscription.
  • Free workshops on grant writing and fundraising.

This membership is free for qualified non-profit organizations.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • Discounted rates on select training programs.
  • Basic listing in the online business directory.
  • Invitation to exclusive member-only events.

Annual Cost: $100

×

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • Priority access to advanced workshops and seminars.
  • Enhanced listing in the online business directory with logo.
  • Opportunities for mentorship programs.
  • One (1) free advertisement slot in the chamber's monthly bulletin.

Annual Cost: $250

×

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver Membership benefits.
  • Exclusive access to VIP networking events with industry leaders.
  • Premium featured placement on the chamber's homepage (spotlight).
  • Personalized business consulting sessions.
  • Discounted rates on all chamber events and services.
  • Unlimited advertisement slots in the chamber's publications.

Annual Cost: $500
